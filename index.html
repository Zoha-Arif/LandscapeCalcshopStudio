Type the name of the color you'd like for your drawing tool.
i.e. purple, blue, brown, yellow.

Type the number of thickness you'd like your drawing tool to
have. The higher the number, the thicker the drawing tool mark.
the default is 2 pixels.

Type an original function. This will form the upper level of
mountains. Remember that all functions are graphed on a coordinate
plane with xMax and yMax of 10 and xMin and yMin of -10.

Type the integral of the original upper mountain function you
entered below. Use the Calcshop Indefinite Integral calculator!
You may use a random value for C or 0. This will form the lower
level of mountains. Remember that all functions are graphed on
a coordinate plane with xMax and yMax of 10 and xMin and yMin
of -10.

Type an original function. This will form the upper roof of your
left-hand cloud. Remember that all functions are graphed on a
coordinate plane with xMax and yMax of 10 and xMin and yMin of -10.

Type the derivative of the original upper mountain function you
entered below. Use the Calcshop Derivative calculator! This will
form the lower level of mountains. Remember that all functions are
graphed on a coordinate plane with xMax and yMax of 10 and xMin
and yMin of -10.

Type an original function.

Type the derivative or integral of the original function entered
above. Use the Calcshop Derivative or Indefinite Integral
calculator! You may use a random value for C or 0 if calculating
an indefinite integral. Remember that all functions are graphed
on a coordinate plane with xMax and yMax of 10 and xMin and yMin
of -10.
